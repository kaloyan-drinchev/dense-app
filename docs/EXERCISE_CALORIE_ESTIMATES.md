# Exercise Calorie Burn Estimates

This document provides approximate calorie burn estimates for all exercises that can be generated by the DENSE AI Program Generator.

## Notes

- Estimates are based on MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) values
- Values assume a **70kg (154lb) person** performing moderate intensity sets
- Calories scale with body weight (heavier people burn more)
- Includes rest time between sets (60-90 seconds)
- Actual calories vary based on:
  - Individual metabolism
  - Intensity/weight lifted
  - Rest periods
  - Form and efficiency

## Important Considerations

1. **EPOC (Excess Post-Exercise Oxygen Consumption)**: After heavy lifting, your body continues burning calories at an elevated rate for hours. This "afterburn" effect can add 10-20% to total calories.

2. **Muscle Building**: While lifting burns calories, building muscle increases your resting metabolic rate long-term.

3. **Intensity Matters**: Heavier weights and shorter rest periods increase calorie burn significantly.

4. **Individual Variation**: These are estimates. Actual calories vary based on:
   - Fitness level
   - Muscle mass
   - Age
   - Hormones
   - Genetics
